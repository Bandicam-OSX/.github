## 🖌 Overview  
Bandicam Advanced is a high-performance screen and game recording application designed to capture video with minimal impact on system performance. Whether you are recording gameplay, tutorials, or professional presentations, it delivers clean, high-quality output.

Hardware-accelerated encoding uses the GPU to compress video in real time, keeping CPU usage low even during demanding recording sessions. Game performance and recording quality do not have to be a trade-off.

Real-time drawing tools let you annotate directly on the screen while recording, highlighting mouse movements, drawing attention arrows, and adding text overlays without pausing or editing the recording afterward.

Webcam overlay positions your camera feed anywhere on the recording frame with adjustable size and opacity, adding a face-cam layer to any content without requiring a second recording setup.

For gamers, content creators, and educators who need reliable, high-quality screen capture with the flexibility to add commentary and annotations in real time, Bandicam Advanced is the recording tool built for the task.

## 🚀 Why Choose Bandicam Advanced?

Bandicam Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets Bandicam Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
